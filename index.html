Game`s rules

Players must build as many patterns on the cards as they can in a round. The player wins in the round if he builds the most number of patterns. The player wins in the game if his amount of built patterns for all rounds is the most, else it is draw. There are n - 1 cards of patterns in a round where n is number of players. As all patterns on cards are built, the round is over. Mechanic of game: you put any marble in free recess on one of four disks of the game field, then you rotate any disk on 90 degrees clockwise or counterclockwise. When you place the marble, no one will able to move it.
